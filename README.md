# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview
This project analyzes business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, profitable categories, and regional sales performance.

## Task
Data Science & Analytics Internship - Task 1  
Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Dataset
Superstore Sales Dataset

## Tool Used
- Power BI Desktop

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales: 2.30M
- Total Profit: 286.40K
- Total Quantity: 38K

## Analysis Performed
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Region
- Profit by Sub-Category
- Sales Trend Over Time
- Top Products by Sales

## Key Insights
1. Technology generated the highest sales among all categories.
2. The West region recorded strong sales performance.
3. Copiers and Phones were among the most profitable sub-categories.
4. Sales showed an increasing trend over the years.
5. A few top products contributed significantly to total sales.

## Recommendations
1. Focus more on high-performing categories such as Technology.
2. Promote profitable sub-categories such as Copiers and Phones.
3. Improve sales strategies in lower-performing regions.
4. Track yearly sales trends for better inventory and marketing planning.
5. Use dashboards regularly to monitor business performance.
